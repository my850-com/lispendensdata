📊 What Are Lis Pendens?

A Lis Pendens is a legal notice filed when a property is involved in a lawsuit. For investors, this is a goldmine of pre-foreclosure opportunities. Be the first to know when properties hit the market before foreclosure auctions.

🎯 Why Choose LisPendensData?

We deliver fresh, accurate data directly from county courthouses. No scraping, no outdated listings — just real-time filings delivered to your inbox daily or weekly. Fresh data means first-mover advantage.

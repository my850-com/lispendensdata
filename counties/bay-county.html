Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Lis Pendens in Bay County?
A Lis Pendens in Bay County is a recorded notice from the Panama City Clerk of Courts indicating property litigation, typically foreclosure proceedings. For agents and investors, this provides early access to distressed properties before they reach public auction.
How do I find pre-foreclosure properties in Bay County?

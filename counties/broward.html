Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Lis Pendens in Broward County
A Lis Pendens is a legal notice filed in Broward County public records indicating a lawsuit concerning property title. This is often the first public indicator that a property is entering foreclosure proceedings. Once filed the Lis Pendens creates a cloud on the property title meaning the property cannot be sold or refinanced until the dispute is resolved.

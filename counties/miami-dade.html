Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Lis Pendens in Miami-Dade County
A Lis Pendens in Miami-Dade is a legal notice recorded with the Clerk of Courts indicating pending litigation against a property. In Miami-Dade's fast-moving real estate market this notice often signals the beginning of foreclosure proceedings or an opportunity for agents and investors to connect with distressed property owners before public auction. Once filed a Lis Pendens attaches to the property title preventing sale or refinance until resolved.

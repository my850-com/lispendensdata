What is a Lis Pendens in Orange County
In Orange County a hub of Orlando real estate activity a Lis Pendens is a public notice recorded with the Clerk of Courts indicating property litigation most commonly foreclosure proceedings. For agents and investors in Orlando Winter Park and Apopka this filing provides advance notice of distressed properties before they reach the auction block. The notice creates a title encumbrance that must be cleared before sale or refinance.
